What I Do

I solve real-world problems through digital solutions. As principal at think design I work with arts, cultural heritage institutions, businesses and universities discovering digital consumer needs and wants, designing new ways to do business, to learn and make product.

How I Do It


Discover
Understand the business, validate user needs & opportunities, research the competitive landscape. Answer essential questions like: how do people actually interact with the brand? Through combinations of focus groups, observation and user tests. Identify essential features.

Build & Validate
Generate original concepts. Test and incorprate findings into next round of prototypes. Refine features, interactions and visual design based on user feedback. Iterate until a powerful, near perfect product has been realised.
