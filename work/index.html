What We Do

Solve real-world problems through digital solutions. At think design we work with arts, cultural heritage institutions, businesses and universities discovering digital consumer needs and wants, designing new ways to do business, to learn and make product.

How We Do It


Discover
Understand the business, validate user needs & opportunities, research the competitive landscape. Answer essential questions like: how do people actually interact with the brand? Through combinations of focus groups, observation and user tests. Identify essential features.

Build & Validate
Generate original concepts. Test and incorporate findings into next round of prototypes. Refine features, interactions and visual design based on user feedback. Iterate until a powerful, near perfect product has been realised.
